Six Flags Great Adventure

Useful Links

Six Flags Great Adventure is an amusement park complex in Jackson, New Jersey. The park, which is owned and operated by Six Flags, is located between New York City and Philadelphia, and is well-known for the water park ride Hurricane Harbor. Under the leadership of Warner LeRoy, it originally opened to the public in 1974 as Great Adventure, and the park was purchased by Six Flags in 1977.
